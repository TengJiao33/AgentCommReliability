## Interpretation

The compact target-state field is not a win in this form.

It does not reduce cost; it increases average communication tokens by `172.7`
tokens/sample over final-only when paired with the final-answer contract. It
also reduces EM and F1.

The useful finding is diagnostic: `Target Slot` compliance is high, but target
slot stability is low. The failure is not that the model refuses the schema. It
is that different turns or agents instantiate different targets.

Sample `199` is the clean sentinel:

- final-only answers `1999 Odisha cyclone` correctly;
- compact target + final alternates target slots between `1999 Odisha cyclone`
  and `Cyclone Gonu`;
- the final answer becomes `Kathantara`, a film title rather than the cyclone.
